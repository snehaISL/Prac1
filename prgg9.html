Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a light,soft and crispy crepe stuffed with a savory,wonderfully spiced potato and onion filling.Tastes best with Sambar and Chutney.

Paneer Butter Masala

This dish is a rich and creamy curry made with paneer(cottage cheese),spices,onions,tomatoes,cashews and butter.The curry is cooked in butter and is absolutely delicious.

Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a dessert consisting of fresh cheese,sugar,crust made of crushed cookies,graham crackers and is finally backed.It is topped with whipped cream,fruits and chocolate syrup.
